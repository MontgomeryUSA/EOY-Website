END OF THE YEAR PRESENTATIONS

ABOUT

On April 20th, the FHS Computer Science department will be hosting its annual End of Year Project exhibition. Groups consisting of students in our CS1, AP CS A, and CS3 classes will get the chance to present their projects in the auditorium. Afterwards, there will be a walking gallery for visitors to see the projects up close.

OUR PURPOSE

The goal of the EOY Project showcase is to allow students to develop skills in teamwork and presentation while also getting to show how they have developed their coding skills throughout the year. Groups are intermixed with students from each level of CS class to allow older students to mentor younger ones.
